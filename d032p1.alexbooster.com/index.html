Countdown from and to timers

The first flip clock/countdown timer is counting from the start of the day
on which I started my 120-day coding challenge (11 September 2017).

The second is counting to the day on which my 120-day coding challenge will end.
i.e. the day 120 of my 120-day challenge will be the 8th January 2018.
So, the counter is counting up to the 9th January 2018.
