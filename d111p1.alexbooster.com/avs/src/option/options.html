Any Video Speed Control allows you to easily control the playback speed of any HTML5 video using keyboard.
Simply press the plus key(+) on your keyboard to increase the playback speed or the minus key/dash(-) to decrease the playback speed.
The multiply key(×) will reset the video speed to normal.
If '+' and '-' keys are already used by another extension or you want to use different keys, you can change that from settings tab.

This extension also works when an HTML5 video player is embedded in another website (like a YouTube video embedded on a third-party website etc). Make sure that the player is in focus. A website can have multiple YouTube players embedded and the keyboard shortcuts will affect only the currently playing ones. For this to work, make sure the desired player is in focus.
